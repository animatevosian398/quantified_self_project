Sound Features & Mood of My Days

Average audio features of my listening habits & my self-recorded mood over 10 days

  • Speechiness - How speechy/wordy is the track? "The more exclusively speech-like the recording (e.g. talk show, audio book, poetry), the closer to 1.0 the attribute value" (Spotify Documentation)
  • Mode - Major (1) vs. minor (0) scale; major keys feel more positive than minor keys.
  • Valence - Measured 0 to 1; "Describing the musical positiveness conveyed by a track." High valence is more cheerful, low valence is more depressed. (Spotify Documentation)
  • Instrumentalness - Measured 0 to 1; Does the track have vocals?
  • Energy - Measured 0 to 1; How energetic is the track? "Perceptual measure of intensity and activity" (Spotify Documentation)
  • Danceability - Measured 0 (least danceable) to 1 (most danceable); How danceable is the track? "Based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity" (Spotify Documentation)
  • Acousticness - 0 to 1; How acoustic is the track?
